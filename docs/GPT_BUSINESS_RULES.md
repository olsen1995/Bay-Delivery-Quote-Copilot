# Business Rules

## Absolute Minimum

- No job below $60 CAD
- Hard global floor

## Travel Minimum

- $20 gas
- $20 wear
= $40 base cost

## Labour

- $20/hr primary
- $16/hr helper

## Service Minimums

- Dump run: $50 (overridden by $60 floor)
- Small move: $60
- Demo: $75

## Scrap

- Included in minimum service charge
- No “free” or “$30” messaging

## Payment

- Cash: no HST
- EMT: +13%

## Philosophy

- Protect margin
- Never undercharge
- Lean mid-to-high when unsure
